This privacy policy will explain how LogicStar uses the personal data we collect from you when you use our website.

What data do we collect?

We do not ask our users to accept cookies and we do not use cookies or collect personal identifiable information (PII) ourselves from this website.

We use Friendly Analytics to measure the usage and reach of our website. We use analytics that do not need to process and store personal identifiable information (PII) and without setting cookies. All data collected and processed by is stored in Switzerland with providers headquartered in Switzerland. Information about the type, scope and purpose of data processing can be found in the privacy policy of Friendly Analytics.

What does it mean for the user?

If you as a user want to contact us, please use the corresponding social media or communication channels. We have the all listed at the bottom of the page.

Providing services.
