Pioneering Autonomous AI for Bug Fixing

Founded by the original creators of DeepCode AI, who successfully sold their product to Snyk and expanded it into a business line worth over $100 million. We are developing a platform to automate the maintenance of commercial applications. Our solution autonomously reproduces and fixes bugs with high accuracy, significantly saving time for engineering teams.
